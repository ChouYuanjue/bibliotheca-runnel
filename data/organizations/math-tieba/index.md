# About Us

Baidu Mathematics Tieba was founded on December 3, 2003. It is one of the largest mathematics forums on the Chinese Internet and is known as one of the four major science academic Tiebas, along with Physics Tieba, Chemistry Tieba, and Biology Tieba.

Before you lies an infinite ocean of mathematics. Are you ready to set sail?

Welcome to the Mathematics Tieba family. Here, mathematics enthusiasts from all over the country gather to discuss mathematical problems covering elementary school, middle school, and university levels. Let's swim and play together in this infinite ocean.
