# About Us

We are the NJU-AIA Association. Our mission is to popularize AI knowledge to more interested students, regardless of whether they are AI majors. We provide introductory guidance on AI theories and practical skills. At the same time, we aim to attract more AI students to form a vibrant AI community.

## Our Goals

*   **Popularize AI Knowledge**
*   **Provide Introductory Guidance**
*   **Form a Vibrant AI Community**
*   **Promote Academic Exchange**
*   **Encourage Practical Innovation**

## Our Activities

### Regular Discussion and Sharing Meetings
Exchange insights on the development direction of the AI field, and discuss practical problems encountered in coursework applications and AI coding.

### Project/Competition Guidance
Select and complete 1 to 2 AI projects/competitions each semester to promote member exchange and cooperation, creating a creative environment.
